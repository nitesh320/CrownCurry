Luxury in every creamy bite

Our creamy risotto is made from perfectly cooked Arborio rice, slowly stirred with a rich medley of wild mushrooms to release their earthy flavors.

Finished with a drizzle of premium truffle oil and a generous sprinkle of aged parmesan, this dish offers a luxurious experience in every bite. Garnished with microgreens, it’s a treat for both your taste buds and eyes. Perfect for those who appreciate refined.

Char-grilled perfection

Tender cubes of fresh paneer are marinated overnight in a special blend of smoky spices and hung curd, then grilled to perfection on open flames.

Served with our house-made tangy mint chutney and roasted bell peppers, this appetizer combines rich smoky flavors with a subtle tang, making it an irresistible start to your meal. Ideal for vegetarians craving bold, vibrant tastes.

Fresh flavors from the sea

Seasonal fish is delicately marinated in extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon, garlic, and Mediterranean herbs, then grilled to perfection to lock in moisture and flavor.

Served with a side of roasted baby vegetables and a light herb-infused sauce, this dish delivers a perfect balance of freshness, zest, and sophisticated taste, reminiscent of the sun-kissed Mediterranean coast.

Colorful, wholesome, and delicious

Vibrant bell peppers are hollowed and filled with a hearty mixture of quinoa, sautéed vegetables, aromatic herbs, and a touch of cheese.

Baked to perfection, each bite is a burst of flavors and textures – soft, crunchy, and savory all at once. Garnished with a drizzle of roasted red pepper sauce, this dish is perfect for health-conscious food lovers without compromising on taste.

A tropical twist on dessert

A luscious dessert that layers creamy cheesecake, fresh mango puree, and lightly crushed buttery biscuit crumbs in an elegant glass.

Served chilled, each spoonful offers a harmonious blend of sweetness, tanginess, and crunch. Topped with mint leaves and a sprinkle of caramelized sugar, this parfait is a refreshing and visually stunning way to end your dining experience.
